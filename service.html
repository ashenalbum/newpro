服务介绍Our Service

海外房屋租赁与购买：客户可直接通过平台看到房源信息，进行在线预约看房和下单。通过网站预约之后可以与客服沟通看房时间，选择线上看房或者线下看房都可以，易居会安排专业的人员带领客户看心仪的房源。

We offer overseas properties rental and purchase service, listing information can be found directly through our website, online booking appointment to view the property or chat with customer service for any queries. Online or offline property viewing appointments can be made.

服务流程Service Process

客户可以在官网线上浏览房源信息之后，选择填表格下单的方式预约客服线上看房，若觉得房源心仪之后便可以预约线下看房的时间。如果客户觉得房源合适便可以签署合同，线上支付定金之后办理入住手续。

Use our official website to search property, fill in the form to make an appointment to view the property online, if you prefer to view the property in person you can make an appointment to view the property with our professional staff. After viewing, if you would like to rent the property, we will sign the tenancy agreement and make your deposit payment online, then you can collect your keys after all the progress done.
